Proposal

Target audience:

My target audience is people who mainly use the internet as their go to news place; generally people aged 15 - 30.

The title / What will I call my news broadcast:

I thought of three names: In the news, News today and Headliner news. After thinking about them I decided 'In the news' was the name I would use as it made most sense because my news broadcast would be about stories in the news.

Headline story:

The headline story needed to be about something local and an achievement so I made a story on a 15 year old girl who got her video into a movie.

Components of my news broadcast:

There is three main parts to my broadcast: Intro/Outro/Continuity sequences, Mainstory and The weather.

The file formats I used are: .aep = Adobe AfterEffects .psd = Adobe Photoshop Document.png = Portable Network Graphic .jpeg = Joint Photographic Experts Group

The programs I used were: Adobe AfterEffects-for animation of weather and Intro, Outro and Continuity sequences, Adobe Photoshop-for all image related work/editing, Adobe PremirePro-for editing all the finished renders/recordings together to make the headline story, Adobe Dreamweaver-for making this website.
